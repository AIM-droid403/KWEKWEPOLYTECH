About Kwekwe Polytechnic

Our History

Kwekwe Polytechnic was established in 1965 as a technical training institute to address the growing need for skilled technicians in Zimbabwe's industrial sector. Originally focusing on engineering trades, the institution has expanded over the decades to include a wide range of technical and vocational programs.

Throughout its history, Kwekwe Polytechnic has maintained a reputation for excellence in technical education, producing graduates who have gone on to make significant contributions to Zimbabwe's development. Our alumni network spans across various industries, both locally and internationally.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality technical and vocational education that equips students with practical skills, knowledge, and values necessary for personal development and contribution to national economic growth. We strive to create an environment that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and ethical professionalism.

We are committed to maintaining high academic standards while adapting our curriculum to meet the evolving needs of industry and society. Through partnerships with businesses and communities, we ensure our programs remain relevant and impactful.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a center of excellence in technical and vocational education, recognized for producing competent, innovative, and entrepreneurial graduates who drive sustainable development in Zimbabwe and beyond.

We envision a future where Kwekwe Polytechnic is the preferred institution for technical education, known for its state-of-the-art facilities, industry-relevant programs, and commitment to student success. We aim to continually expand our offerings to include emerging technologies and fields.
